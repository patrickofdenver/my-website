Frequently Asked Questions

  • help_outline How do I download your apps?

    Our apps are available on the Google Play Store for Android devices. Visit our Apps page to find download links. iOS versions are coming soon!

  • security Do your apps collect my personal data?

    No. We prioritize your privacy and do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All app functionality works entirely on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

  • attach_money Are your apps free?

    Yes! Our current apps are completely free to download and use, with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements. We believe helpful tools should be accessible to everyone.

  • update How often do you update your apps?

    We regularly release updates to improve functionality, fix bugs, and add new features based on user feedback. Make sure to enable automatic updates in your app store settings to get the latest versions.

  • bug_report I found a bug. How do I report it?

    Please email us at support@neuralhound.com with details about the bug, including your device type, app version, and steps to reproduce the issue. We appreciate your help in making our apps better!

  • feedback Can I request new features?

    Absolutely! We love hearing from users about features they'd like to see. Send your suggestions to support@neuralhound.com. While we can't implement every request, user feedback directly influences our development roadmap.

  • wifi_off Do your apps work offline?

    Yes! Our apps are designed to work completely offline. Once downloaded, you don't need an internet connection to use any features.

  • smartphone What devices are compatible with your apps?

    Our Android apps are compatible with devices running Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher. iOS versions are in development and will support iOS 13 and above when released.
