Respiratory Assistant

About This App

Respiratory Assistant is designed to help users to perform calculations necessary for the field of respiratory therapy. Included in the app are calculations for initial ventilator settings, compliance calculations, ABG analysis, and shunt calculations.

The app is straightforward and easy to use, with no complicated setup or learning curve. Just open it and start your calculations.
