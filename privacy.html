Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

verified_user Quick Summary

Neural Hound applications do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All app functionality operates entirely on your device. We respect your privacy and have designed our applications to work without requiring access to your personal data.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Neural Hound, Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use for all applications developed and published by Neural Hound, Ltd., including but not limited to the Respiratory Assistant app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our applications are designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Health or medical information
  • Photos, media, or files from your device
  • Contacts or social media information
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

3. How Our Apps Work

All functionality in Neural Hound applications operates entirely on your device. Any settings, preferences, or data generated within the app are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. We have no access to this information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies. However, when you download our apps from app stores (Google Play Store, Apple App Store), those platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies, which are separate from ours.

5. Permissions

Some of our apps may request device permissions (such as notifications or audio for breathing exercise cues). These permissions are used solely for app functionality on your device and do not enable us to collect any data. You can review and modify permissions at any time in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. Because we collect no data whatsoever, our apps are safe for users of all ages. However, we recommend parental guidance for children using any mobile application.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect, transmit, or store any user data, there is no data that could be compromised in a security breach. All app data remains on your device and is protected by your device's own security measures.

8. Communication

If you contact us via email for support or inquiries (at support@neuralhound.com or business@neuralhound.com), we will use your email address and any information you provide solely to respond to your request. We do not add you to mailing lists or share your contact information with third parties. Email communications are voluntary and initiated by you.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of significant changes through app store update notes or by posting an updated policy on this website. Your continued use of our applications after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Your Rights

Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for you to access, correct, or delete. You maintain complete control over your information since it never leaves your device. You can uninstall our apps at any time to remove all locally stored app data from your device.

11. International Users

Our applications can be used worldwide. Since we do not collect any data, there are no data transfer or international privacy concerns. All processing occurs on your local device.

12. Legal Basis (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Union, we note that we do not process any personal data as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our apps are designed to function without collecting or processing personal data, thus GDPR obligations related to data processing do not apply.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents: We do not sell personal information, because we do not collect any personal information. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have certain rights, but since we collect no data, there is no personal information to access, delete, or opt out of sharing.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Neural Hound, Ltd.
Email: support@neuralhound.com
Website: www.neuralhound.com

15. Acknowledgment

By downloading and using any Neural Hound application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Bottom line: We built our apps to respect your privacy completely. We don't collect anything, we don't track anything, and we don't share anything. Your data stays on your device, period.
